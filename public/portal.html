Legal Commitment — Read Before Submitting
By listing your vehicle on PrivaBuy, you are entering a legally binding exclusivity agreement. Please read the following carefully:
1
Your car is exclusively PrivaBuy's to sell while the auction is live. Once you submit, you may not list, advertise, or sell this vehicle on Facebook Marketplace, Craigslist, CarMax, to a private buyer, or through any other channel — for any price — while your auction is active. Dealers are bidding in good faith. Selling elsewhere is a breach of this agreement.
2
If you accept a dealer's bid, you must complete the sale with that dealer. Accepting a bid is a binding commitment to transfer the vehicle to the winning dealer. You may not change your mind, sell to someone else, or back out after accepting. The vehicle belongs to the winning dealer from the moment you accept.
3
If you accept a bid and fail to deliver the vehicle, you forfeit your $250 platform fee. The fee is non-refundable in all cases of seller non-performance. PrivaBuy reserves the right to pursue additional damages on behalf of the affected dealer, including recovery of any costs incurred by the dealer in preparation for the transaction.
Once you submit, your auction goes live immediately. 5 local dealers are notified. Your auction stays open with no time limit — accept whenever you're ready.
Terms & Conditions — Seller Agreement
1. Accuracy of Vehicle Information I confirm that all information submitted about my vehicle is true, complete, and accurate to the best of my knowledge. Misrepresentation may result in cancellation of any accepted offer without recourse.

2. Auction & Offer Terms I understand that listing my vehicle on PrivaBuy initiates a live auction. I am not obligated to accept any bid. Once I accept a bid, I am entering a binding agreement to sell at the accepted price.

3. Platform Fee Upon accepting an offer, a one-time platform fee of $250 is due to PrivaBuy before dealer contact information is released. This fee is non-refundable once processed.

4. No Time Limit My auction has no mandatory expiration. It remains open until I choose to accept or close it.

5. Data & Privacy My contact information will only be shared with the winning dealer after I accept their bid and the platform fee is paid.

Getting Started

PrivaBuy connects you with local dealers who bid on your vehicle in a live auction. Here's the full flow, top to bottom:

1. List Vehicle
Fill out a 5-step form describing your car. Takes about 5 minutes.
2. My Auction
Your auction goes live immediately. Dealers place bids in real time. You accept whenever you're ready — no deadline.
3. Deal Flow
Once you accept a bid, a step-by-step checklist walks both you and the dealer through the Bill of Sale, pickup scheduling, inspection, and payment.
4. History
See all your past listings, bid counts, and final sale prices in one table.
List Vehicle

A 5-step wizard. You must complete each step before moving to the next.

Step 1 — Vehicle
  • VIN Number + Decode VIN button: Enter your 17-character VIN and click Decode VIN to auto-fill Year, Make, Model, and Trim from the NHTSA database.
  • Year / Make / Model / Trim / Color / Mileage: Basic vehicle details — all required.
  • Transmission / Drivetrain pills: Tap to select one option each.
  • Title Status pills: Clean, Salvage, Rebuilt, or Lien Present.
  • Loan or Lease pills: If you select Loan or Lease, a lien section appears asking for your lienholder, 10-day payoff amount, and a payoff statement upload — required before you can continue.
  • Add-ons / Upgrades: Free text — list anything or write N/A.
  • ZIP Code / Two or More Keys pills.
  • "Check Eligibility & Continue" button: Validates all fields, shows a pass/fail card based on your vehicle's age and mileage, then advances you to Step 2 automatically.
Step 2 — Photos
  • 6 required photo slots: Front Exterior, Rear Exterior, Driver Side, Passenger Side, Interior/Dash, Odometer.
  • 2 optional slots: Engine Bay, Any Damage.
  • Tap any slot to open your device's file picker.
  • "Continue" button: Disabled logic-wise until all 6 required photos are uploaded.
Step 3 — Condition
  • Exterior / Interior / Mechanical tabs: Three sub-sections you complete in order.
  • Exterior: Click the car diagram (Front/Side/Rear views) to mark specific damage points, plus condition pills (scratches, dents, rust, etc.), tires replaced, windshield condition.
  • Interior: Odor, stains, rips, dashboard damage cards; moonroof status; smoked-in status.
  • Mechanical: Warning lights, mechanical issues, driveability, accident history.
  • Overall Condition pills and Additional Comments free text apply to the whole vehicle.
  • Truthfulness confirmation: Required "Yes" selection before continuing.
  • Back / Continue buttons: Move between the three sub-tabs, then to Step 4.
Step 4 — Value & Price
  • Shows an estimated trade-in value pulled from real market listings when available.
  • Set Your Minimum Price (floor price) input: The lowest bid you'll accept. Dealers never see this number — it's for your protection only.
  • Live feedback tells you if your floor is too low, too high, or well-calibrated.
Step 5 — Review
  • Summarizes your vehicle, minimum price, and disclosures.
  • Exclusivity agreement checkbox: You're agreeing not to sell the car elsewhere while the auction is live.
  • Terms & Conditions checkbox: Confirms your listing information is accurate.
  • "Start My Auction — It's Free" button: Stays disabled until both checkboxes are checked. Submitting goes live immediately and notifies local dealers.
My Auction

Once your listing goes live, this tab shows the real-time auction.

  • Elapsed timer: Shows how long your auction has been open. There's no deadline — it stays open until you accept a bid.
  • Live Bids feed: Every dealer offer appears here, anonymized as "Dealer A," "Dealer B," etc. The top bid is highlighted.
  • Your Vehicle card: A summary of what you listed, plus your floor price.
  • "Accept Top Bid" button: Disabled until at least one bid comes in. Clicking it opens a confirmation — accepting is binding and starts the Deal Flow.
  • "Counter Offer" button: Lets you propose a higher amount to the current top bidder, who then has a window to respond.
  • If the top bid is below your floor price, a warning banner appears — you're never required to accept a bid below your floor.
History

A table of every listing you've ever created, past or present.

  • Vehicle: Year/Make/Model and mileage.
  • Date: When the listing was created.
  • Bids: Total number of offers received.
  • Top Offer: The highest bid the auction reached.
  • Floor: The minimum price you set for that listing.
  • Status: Open (live), Won/Accepted, or Closed.
Deal Flow

After you accept a bid, this tab appears and walks you through completing the sale, step by step.

  1. Confirm Win: The dealer confirms their win and signs a facilitation acknowledgement.
  2. Bill of Sale: Both you and the dealer must check a box agreeing to the terms of sale before moving forward.
  3. Schedule Pickup: The dealer proposes 3 possible pickup times.
  4. Confirm Pickup Time: You pick whichever of the 3 times works best for you.
  5. Pickup Day: On the scheduled day, the dealer confirms the vehicle matches your listing (or flags a discrepancy).
  6. 24hr Inspection: If the vehicle has disclosed accident history, the dealer may take extra time to inspect before paying.
  7. Title Transfer: The dealer must upload a photo of the signed title before they're allowed to pay you — this protects you from handing over the car without payment.
  8. Seller Confirms Payment: Once the dealer pays, you confirm receipt and hand over the title.
  9. Complete: The deal is finished.
Note: A $350 PrivaBuy facilitation fee is deducted from the dealer's payment to you — you never pay anything out of pocket. Your Deal Flow tab always shows what you'll actually receive after this fee.
